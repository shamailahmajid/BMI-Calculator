# BMI Calculator

A Python program that calculates Body Mass Index (BMI).

## Features
- Accepts weight in kilograms (kgs) or pounds (lbs)
- Accepts height in meters (m) or inches (in)
- Automatically converts units when needed
- Calculates and displays BMI
- 
## Example

What is your name?
John

What is your weight?
70

Is your weight in kilograms (kgs) or pounds (lbs)?
kgs

What is your height?
1.75

Is your height in meters (m) or inches (in)?
m

Your BMI is 22.86
